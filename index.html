ABOUT BRGY. PUGUIS

Barangay Puguis is one of the largest barangay in the municipality of La Trinidad, Benguet. Puguis is a home of the Ibalois, they originally founded in the valley particularly Puguis and Pico. The District of Benguet was created at Pukis(Puguis) and La Trinidad was made by the "Commandancia Politico Militares de Benguet".

VISION

We envision Barangay Puguis a balanced, self-sustaining agricultural, and well-developed community with God-fearing, healthy and united people living within.
