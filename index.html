Advocacies


Barangay puguis either collaborates with external organizations or work solely to manage the state of the barangay. They have two advocacies towards managing and promoting the well-being of the PWDs in the community, livelihood programs and medical missions. these advocacies are held by the barangay quarterly or as needed.


Livelihood Programs

Livelihood programs are held in barangay puguis to help improve people's way of life there. The officials of the barangay collaborate with the DSWD and TESDA organizations to conduct workshops to impart their knowledge about livelihood activities such as handicrafting, massaging, etc. Interested community members especially the PWDs attend these programs in order to be trained, not only to learn new skills to improve their livelihood by being introduced to profit-making activities.

Medical Mission

When medical help is needed, they provide it. The barangay officials along with the members of the Nazarene Church work with each other to provide medical help to the people of barangay puguis. They diligently check-up on families from every door in the community to see how they are doing, especially to take care of their PWDs.

PWD Rights


Persons with disability(PWD) posses rights that are mandated by the government. These rights exist to cater their special needs may it be financial, architectural, and others, and to protect them from the possible threats of society. Such as privileges like discounts, priorities, etc.

About Barangay Puguis


Barangay Puguis

Barangay Puguis Barangay Puguis is one of the largest barangay in the municipality of La Trinidad, Benguet. Puguis is a home of the Ibalois, they originally founded in the valley particularly Puguis and Pico. The District of Benguet was created at Pukis(Puguis) and La Trinidad was made by the "Commandancia Politico Militares de Benguet".


Mission

To unite the people within the Barangay and promote active participation towards achieving our vision. To exercise the Council’s maximum taxing powers granted by the Local Code. To partner experts in the conduct of programs and activities benefiting the whole community.

Vision

We envision Barangay Puguis a balanced, self-sustaining agricultural, and well-developed community with God-fearing, healthy and united people living within.
